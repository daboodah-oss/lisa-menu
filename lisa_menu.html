🏆 Best Picks
🥗
Chicken Salad Chick
✅ Best Overall
Order: Classic Carol on white bread (plain chicken salad, no chips). Olivia's Old South also fine.
⚠️ Skip: Fancy Nancy/Cranberry Kelli (nuts), any spicy or bacon varieties, chips on the side
DoorDash Order Online
🥣
McAlister's Deli
✅ Best Soups
Order: Half turkey sandwich on white + cup of chicken noodle soup (Choose 2 combo). Roast turkey sandwich plain also great.
⚠️ Skip: McAlister's Club (has bacon), potato bacon soup, any loaded spud, broccoli cheddar (high fat)
DoorDash Order Online
🍞
Panera Bread
✅ Best Soups
Order: Cup of chicken noodle soup. Turkey sandwich on white or sourdough, no mayo, no extras. Open at 6 AM.
⚠️ Skip: Bread bowls, salads (raw veg/nuts), bacon items, avocado, broccoli cheddar soup (high fat)
DoorDash Order Online
🍗 Fast Food — Good Options
🐔
Chick-fil-A
✅ Best Fast Food
Order: Grilled Nuggets 8-pc (130 cal, 3g fat — the single best fast food item). Grilled Chicken Sandwich, no pickle, no sauce.
⚠️ Skip: Classic (fried) nuggets, anything spicy, mac & cheese side, milkshakes, waffle fries
DoorDash App / Order
🍔
Wendy's
✅ Good
Order: Grilled Chicken Sandwich (no mayo). Plain Baked Potato (no toppings or light butter only). Jr. Hamburger plain.
⚠️ Skip: Frosty (ice cream — not allowed), Baconator, Dave's Double, any spicy option, chili (beans = high fiber)
DoorDash App / Order
🍟
McDonald's
✅ Good
Order: Plain Hamburger (ketchup/mustard only, no cheese). Grilled Chicken Sandwich. Hotcakes plain (breakfast, no sausage).
⚠️ Skip: Big Mac, Quarter Pounder, McNuggets (fried), McFlurry/sundae, hash browns, sausage, bacon
DoorDash App / Order
🥖
Subway
✅ Good if careful
Order: 6" Turkey Breast or Oven-Roasted Chicken on WHITE bread. NOT toasted. Light mayo or mustard only — no raw veggie add-ons.
⚠️ Skip: Any toasted sub (makes bread crunchy), Italian, meatball marinara, oil & vinegar, peppers/onions/pickles
DoorDash App / Order
🥪
Potbelly
✅ Good if careful
Order: Turkey Breast or Chicken Salad on white bread — ask for UNTOASTED (they normally toast). Light mayo or mustard only.
⚠️ Skip: All toasted sandwiches, meatball sub, Italian, any with peppers or hot peppers
DoorDash App / Order
🍔
Culver's
✅ Good
Order: Plain single ButterBurger — no cheese, ketchup/mustard only. Soft bun, lean patty. Mirrors hospital-approved hamburger.
⚠️ Skip: Cheese curds (fried), fish fry (fried), custard / ice cream (not allowed), anything fried
Order / Carryout
🍔
Five Guys
✅ Good
Order: Plain Hamburger — no cheese, no bacon. Ketchup, mustard, lettuce, tomato OK. Soft bun.
⚠️ Skip: Bacon burger, cheese, fries (fried)
Order / Carryout
⚠️ Order Carefully
🥩
Arby's
⚠️ Caution
Only safe order: Plain Classic Roast Beef — no Arby's sauce, no horseradish. Small size. Lean meat on a soft bun.
⚠️ Skip: Beef & Cheddar (cheese not allowed), curly fries (fried + spiced), French Dip, Market Fresh sandwiches, loaded fries
DoorDash App / Order
🍕 Pizza & Pasta
🍝
Chicago's Pizza (Fishers)
✅ Pasta is best
Best order: Spaghetti with marinara sauce — plain, no meatballs, no added cheese. Matches hospital-approved pasta. Also: thin crust plain cheese pizza, 1-2 slices only.
⚠️ Skip: Deep dish (too much fat), any meat toppings, extra cheese, garlic bread (buttery), stuffed pizza
DoorDash Order / Carryout
🍕
Domino's / Pizza Hut
⚠️ Pizza: caution only
If ordering pizza: Thin crust only, plain cheese, light sauce, 1-2 slices max. Domino's Hand Tossed with light cheese is acceptable occasionally.
⚠️ Skip: Pan / deep dish, extra cheese, any meat toppings, stuffed crust, dipping sauces, bread sides
